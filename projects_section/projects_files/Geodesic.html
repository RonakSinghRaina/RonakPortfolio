The Geodesic Equation

This equation describes the path $x^\mu(\tau)$ of a freely falling particle through spacetime.

$$ \frac{d^2 x^\mu}{d\tau^2} + \Gamma^\mu_{\alpha\beta} \frac{dx^\alpha}{d\tau} \frac{dx^\beta}{d\tau} = 0 $$

The Christoffel Symbols ($\Gamma^\mu_{\alpha\beta}$) encode all the information about the curvature of spacetime.
